Online Treasure Hunt: An Exquisite Test of Your Feeble Wits

Rules and Regulations

  • The trial will unfold across five excruciatingly demanding rounds.
  • For each round, a separate Discord server will be conjured to relay the necessary details. An overarching server will also be established, where participants will be summoned once their team leaders deem them worthy. All crucial information regarding the contest shall be disseminated here.
  • Each round shall be heralded by a “clue”—an intricate puzzle designed to tax your limited intellect. Solving the clue will require you to submit your answer on a designated webpage. Should you succeed, you will be granted access to the link for the subsequent round’s server.
  • Hints will be made available after a specific period. Use of these hints will, naturally, result in a forfeiture of points.
  • For certain rounds, the opportunity to “purchase” tools to facilitate your progress will be offered. However, be forewarned: these aids come at a steep and unforgiving cost in points.
DISCLAIMER: We accept no responsibility for any psychological strain this endeavor may impose upon you. Enjoy your suffering.
